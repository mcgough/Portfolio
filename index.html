About Me

I'm a web developer, with experience in both Node.js and Rails as well as relational and non-relational databases. My interests lie in building responsive and dynamic apps using modern frontend frameworks and building eye-catching CSS3 animations. When I’m not immersed in a feature update or learning a new technology, you’ll find me outdoors, climbing, fly fishing, or catching a Mariners game.

Personal Projects

Schedulacity

Manage. Track. Schedule.
Schedulacity was built by two of my classmates and myself. We implemented Google's Calendar API, visualizing it with FullCalendar, and used Twilio to give user's the ability to communicate with their students via text.

DogParkFinder

A Node app, built with Express and Postgres, DogParkFinder utilitizes FourSquare's API and Google Maps, to allow users to locate and checkin at their favorite dog parks.

FullCountNews

FullCount utilizes Node,Sails.js, and MongoDB on the backend and Angular.js on the frontend. The app allows users to get up to the minute MLB news and create a Lineup, which lets them get personalized news on their favorite players

MovieCouch

A simple Node app built with Express, Postgres, and Bootstrap. Users can save their favorite movies and comment on them.
